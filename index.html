Welcome To Adarsh School
Adarsh Sr. Sec. School, Tosham is a beautiful, co-educational, English medium day school with strength of about 1500 students. Affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education,New Delhi. The school is run by Mr. J.C.Malwal. He is the Chairman of Adarsh Sr. Sec. School since its existence. The school is situated amidst lush green congenial surroundings away from the pollution of city life. The school building incorporates the latest in layout and design. Its campus is spread over an area of 5 acres of land.In order to groom the finer skills in children, various laboratories like English lab, Maths lab were introduced which have added glory to the quality of Education imported in school..
Chairperson's Message
ith the great blessing of God and Co-operation of the inhabitants of Tosham, Adarsh Sr. Sec. School was established by me in 1975. But with your best wishes and co-operation, I overcome all the difficulties and the school got "Name and Fame" in the field of education during the last 39 years.
